Inroducing SmartBox !

Building Management Revolution !

SmartBox is a revolutionary building controlling application which allow you to monitor your house or your company anytime anywhere ! SmartBox let you monitor your surveillance camera, intrusion detection system, fire alarm, heating system, and much much more !

This is the MVP page of the Tunisian 7th Team.

SmartBox Features

Surveillance Camera Monitoring

Parent's favorite security feature is of course provided by SBOX. With one touch you can see your house surrounding in real-time any place any time !

Intrusion Detection System

With SBOX notification system you are vigilant 24/24, 7/7, and able to detect any intrusion into your house and immediately take appropriate actions.

Heating/Cooling Control

Luxury is not that far ! with SBOX you can prepare your bath while you're driving home back from a tiring workday. You can also control heat level and the air-conditioner.

Doors Locking

Always wondering if you have firmly closed the door after it's too late ? No more worries ! With a click of a button you can open or close any door you want, or even check it's status and if anybody has opened it without letting you know.

Fire Alarm

We can't talk about safety if we don't consider fire detection. SBOX is able to detect fire or gas leak and alert you and even call fire-fighters
